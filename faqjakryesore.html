Football Club Barcelona commonly referred to as Barcelona and also known as Barça, is a professional football club based in Barcelona, Spain, that competes in La Liga

Founded in 1899 by a group of Swiss, Spanish, German and English footballers led by Joan Gamper, the club has become a symbol of Catalan culture and Catalanism, hence the motto "Més que un club" ("More than a club").It is the fourth-most valuable sports team in the world, worth $4.76 billion, and the world's richest football club in terms of revenue, with an annual turnover of €715.1 million.

Domestically, Barcelona has won a record 75 trophies:

  • 26x La Liga
  • 31x Copa del Rey
  • 13x Supercopa de España
  • 3x Copa Eva Duarte
  • 2x Copa de la Liga titles

In international club football, the club has won twenty European and worldwide titles:

  • 5x UEFA Champions League titles
  • A record four UEFA Cup Winners' Cups
  • A joint record five UEFA Super Cups
  • A record three Inter-Cities Fairs Cups
  • 3x FIFA Club World Cups

In 2009, Barcelona became the first Spanish club to win the continental treble consisting of La Liga,Copa del Rey, and the Champions League, and also became the first Spanish football club to win six out of six competitions in a single year, by also winning the Spanish Super Cup, Super Cup, and FIFA Club World Cup.
